The UNICEF organization (2024) mentions that contaminated drinking water, poor sanitation, and hygiene-related diseases cause millions of deaths annually, particularly among young children. These diseases cause malnutrition and stunting. An example could be how a digestive illness diarrhea can produce 300,000 deaths annually from this alone. Despite advancements, 2.2 billion people worldwide lack access to safe drinking water systems and 4.2 billion people lack access to well-run sanitation systems.
