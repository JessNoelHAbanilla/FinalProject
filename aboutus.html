About Us

Mission and Vision: HygieneC is a forward-thinking organization dedicated to enhancing public health through comprehensive hygiene and sanitation initiatives. Recognizing that proper hygiene is fundamental to disease prevention and overall well-being, HygieneC focuses on promoting healthy behaviors, improving access to sanitation facilities, and fostering community engagement. HygieneC's mission is to empower individuals and communities to adopt sustainable hygiene practices that lead to healthier lives. Its vision is a world where everyone has access to clean water, safe sanitation, and the knowledge to maintain personal and environmental hygiene.


Core Programs and Services:

* Community Education and Awareness to Clean Water and Sanitation

* Infrastructure Development with Proper Plumbing Techniques

* Advocacy and Policy Influence (includes the newly establishes PFP)

* Emergency Response and Relief through Poor Sanitation Related Disasters


Impact and Achievements: HygieneC has reached thousands of individuals through its educational programs, leading to improved hygiene practices and a reduction in water-based diseases. Furthermore, the organization's infrastructure projects have provided access to clean water and sanitation facilities for communities who were previously lacking these essential services. Through its advocacy efforts, HygieneC has contributed to the formulation of policies such as "Water of the Mass" and "PFP" that prioritize hygiene and sanitation in national development plans.


Future Directions: Looking ahead, HygieneC aims to expand its reach by partnering with international organizations and leveraging technology to enhance its programs. The organization plans to introduce mobile applications for hygiene education and data collection, ensuring that its services are adaptable to the evolving needs of communities. And so, HygieneC stands as a beacon of hope and a catalyst for change in the realm of public health, committed to creating a cleaner, healthier, and more equitable world for all.
